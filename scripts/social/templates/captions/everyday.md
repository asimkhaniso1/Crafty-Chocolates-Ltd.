# Occasion: everyday

Default voice. Use when no specific season is active.

Suggested angles to pick from (vary across posts):
- Highlight the chocolate type (milk / dark / dairy-free) or the filling
- Pair the product with a moment: a coffee break, a thank-you to a colleague, a small "kuch meetha" surprise
- Talk about the craft: handmade in Karachi, Belgian-grade chocolate, finished by hand
- Mention the price quietly only if it's a clear value (e.g. Rs. 1,650 for 5 pieces)

Avoid:
- Saying it's "perfect for any occasion" (too generic)
- Pitching it as a "treat for yourself" (we are a gifting brand)
- Mentioning calories, diets, or weight loss
