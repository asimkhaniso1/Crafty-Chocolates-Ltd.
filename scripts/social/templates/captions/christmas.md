# Occasion: christmas

Active 1-31 December. Karachi has an active Christian community and the
Christmas/New Year corporate gifting market is strong across all religions.

Suggested angles:
- Christmas gift hampers for clients and family
- The Christmas bar with assorted fillings (orange, mint, coffee-caramel, oreo)
- Year-end thank-you gifts for clients and staff
- New Year's hosting — chocolates on the dessert spread

Voice notes:
- "Merry Christmas" + "Happy New Year" are appropriate
- Inclusive framing — many buyers are not Christian; lean into "season of giving"
- Urdu phrases that fit: "khaas aapke liye", "tohfa", "dil se"
