# Occasion: corporate

Active during fiscal year-end gifting peaks (Nov-Dec, Jun-Jul) and on
"Anytime" weeks when explicitly featuring custom-logo products.

Suggested angles:
- Custom-molded chocolate bars with the client's logo
- Branded box wraps with the company logo and a personal note
- MOQ 10 boxes — call this out matter-of-factly, not apologetically
- B2B angle: client appreciation, employee onboarding gifts, conference giveaways
- Lead time: mention if it's a few-day turnaround for typical custom orders

Voice notes:
- Less playful, more confident — buyers here are HR / marketing / EAs
- Avoid emojis entirely in this occasion
- Urdu phrases: optional and sparse — corporate captions can be 100% English
