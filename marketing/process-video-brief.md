# Crafty Chocolates — "Raw to Real" Process Video Brief

A production brief for the from-scratch process film. **Shoot this with real footage of your own facility** — its entire value is authenticity (real hygiene, real HACCP, real craft). Do not fake or AI-generate the process shots.

## Two deliverables from one shoot

1. **Hero film (~60s)** — the full journey, raw → finished, for the website (Custom Molds / Our Story) and pinned social.
2. **Stage clips (8 × 6–10s)** — one per stage, vertical 9:16, for Reels / TikTok / Stories. Same footage, cut short.

Shoot everything **both** 16:9 (horizontal, for the hero + website) and 9:16 (vertical, for social) where possible, or shoot 4K horizontal and crop.

---

## Golden rules (authenticity = the whole point)

- Real hands, real facility, real gloves/hairnets. No staged "lab" that isn't yours.
- **Hygiene is woven through every stage, not bolted on the end** — gloves, hairnets, clean stainless surfaces, sanitised tools in frame constantly.
- Show the **HACCP / ISO-9001** certificate or badge on a wall/document at least once, in focus.
- Good light + a tripod or gimbal. Macro/close-up shots of chocolate are the money shots.
- **Do NOT film:** the printed-chocolate setup (holding that back), exact machine brand plates/supplier labels, or anything you wouldn't want a competitor to copy verbatim. Show the craft, not the parts list.

## Shot list (raw → real chocolate)

| # | Stage | Shots to get | Hygiene / HACCP beat |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Raw** | Couverture blocks / cocoa, ingredients in food-safe sealed storage; a hand (gloved) selecting | Labelled, dated storage; sealed containers |
| 2 | **Melt & temper** | Chocolate melting, glossy ribbon pour, thermometer/temper check | Clean stainless, sanitised tools, gloves |
| 3 | **Mould-making** | Design sketch on screen → **laser-cutting** the mould (sparks/precision) → **vacuum-forming** the sheet | Clean workstation; this is the craft hero shot |
| 4 | **Pour & set** | Chocolate poured into the fresh mould; tap-to-settle; into cooling | Clean pour area; covered setting trays |
| 5 | **The reveal** | Demoulding — the finished piece popping out crisp and shiny (SLOW MOTION) | — (this is the "wow" beat) |
| 6 | **Finishing** | Emboss/logo detail macro; foil-wrapping a bite; butter-paper message being placed | Gloved hands throughout |
| 7 | **Boxing** | Pieces set into a box (4+1 / 9+1 / tin), ribbon tied, lid closed | Clean packing station |
| 8 | **QC + certificate** | A final inspection glance; the HACCP/ISO certificate in focus; finished boxed product hero | The trust close |
