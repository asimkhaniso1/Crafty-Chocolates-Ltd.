# Occasion: eid

Active 14 days before and through Eid al-Fitr or Eid al-Adha.

Suggested angles:
- Eidi gifting: chocolates as a modern Eidi for children and adults
- Hosting: a box on the chai trolley when relatives visit
- Corporate Eid gifts for clients and staff
- Personalized Eid bars with the recipient's name on the wrapper

Voice notes:
- "Eid Mubarak" is the safe, universal greeting
- Tone is celebratory and family-warm, not corporate
- Urdu phrases that fit: "dil se", "pyaar bhara tohfa", "khaas aapke liye"
