# Occasion: wedding

Active in shaadi seasons (Oct-Nov, Feb-Apr). Karachi weddings are
multi-event productions — mehndi, baraat, walima — and chocolates show
up on the favor table at each one.

Suggested angles:
- Wedding favors (we can do logos / couple names molded on the chocolate)
- Engagement / nikkah / mehndi giveaways with custom box wraps
- Bulk corporate-style ordering (MOQ 10 boxes — call it out subtly when relevant)
- Save-the-date or thank-you boxes for the bridal party

Voice notes:
- The buyer here is often the bride, her mother, or the wedding planner —
  speak to *thoughtful detail*, not generic romance
- Avoid "tying the knot" and other tired wedding phrases
- Urdu phrases that fit: "dil se", "pyaar bhara tohfa", "meethi yaadein"
