# Occasion: ramadan

Active in the ~30 days leading up to and through Ramadan. The Ramadan
collection centres on chocolate-covered dates stuffed with almond or cashew,
finished with coconut — gifted at iftar gatherings and to clients.

Suggested angles:
- Iftar gifting: a sweet to share when the fast opens
- Sehri / post-tarawih indulgence (lightly — don't push as routine food)
- Corporate Ramadan hampers for clients and team
- The dates: Aseel dates from Pakistan, our own filling

Voice notes:
- Respectful of the spiritual context — never frame the chocolate as
  competing with iftar food or as the "main event"
- "Ramadan Kareem" / "Ramadan Mubarak" are appropriate greetings if
  the post date falls inside the holy month
- Urdu phrases that fit naturally here: "kuch meetha ho jaye", "tohfa",
  "khaas aapke liye"
