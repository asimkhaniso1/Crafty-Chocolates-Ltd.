# Corporate outreach — target segments & starter list (Karachi-first)

Work through one segment per week, 20 contacts/week, using the Track A scripts
in CONTENT-PACK.md. Lead product: **logo bites (Rs 770/box, MOQ 50)** with the
good/better/best ladder behind it (25g bar → 50g bar → bespoke plaque).

## Segment priority (by fit and deal size)

### 1. Real estate & construction (best fit — proven: Archistruct testimonial)
Client-gifting culture, big launch events, high per-deal value.
Targets: project marketing firms, developers, architecture studios —
e.g. Dolmen Group, TPL Properties, HMR Waterfront, Emaar Pakistan (Karachi office),
local architecture/design studios. Pitch: launch-event giveaways with the
project logo molded in chocolate; bespoke plaques for handover ceremonies.

### 2. Banks & financial services (year-end + Eid budgets, repeat annual orders)
Targets: Meezan, HBL, UBL, Bank Alfalah, Faysal — branch/marketing depts;
also insurance (EFU, Jubilee) and brokerages. Pitch: client appreciation
boxes; Eid corporate pre-orders; branded bites for branch openings.
Note: procurement-driven — get on their vendor list early (Sept for Dec budgets).

### 3. Marketing/ad/event agencies (multiplier — they buy for many clients)
Targets: IAL Saatchi, Ogilvy Pakistan, Adcom, Bond Advertising, event
management companies (Jasmine Events, etc.). Pitch: white-label/OEM — "we
make and wrap, you brand for your clients"; fast 48h mock-ups for pitches.

### 4. Hospitals, pharma & clinics (thank-you culture, doctor gifting)
Targets: Aga Khan, Liaquat National, South City, pharma reps (Getz, Searle,
Martin Dow). Pitch: branded bites for conferences and doctor visits — small,
compliant-friendly gift value, high volume.

### 5. Schools & universities (events all year: graduations, sports days)
Targets: private school networks (Beaconhouse, City School, Generations),
universities (IBA, LUMS Karachi events, Habib). Pitch: crest/logo chocolates
for graduations and annual days; bulk pricing sells itself.

### 6. Tech companies & startups (employee-experience budgets, LinkedIn-reachable)
Targets: Systems Ltd, 10Pearls, Folio3, Bazaar, Abhi, banks' digital arms.
Pitch: onboarding kits, work-anniversary chocolates, Eid boxes for remote teams.

### 7. Hotels & restaurants (recurring OEM, not one-off)
Targets: Movenpick, Marriott, Avari, Pearl Continental, boutique cafés.
Pitch: turndown/after-dinner chocolates with the hotel monogram — monthly
standing order (wholesale rates, they handle presentation).

## Weekly rhythm
- Mon: 20 new contacts from the current segment (LinkedIn + info@ emails + WhatsApp where known)
- Attach: logo-reveal video + one-line pitch from CONTENT-PACK
- Fri: follow-ups due (Day 3/7/14 touches)
- Track in a simple sheet: company / contact / segment / date / stage / sample sent?

## Rules
- Always offer the Rs 500 sample box — it converts better than any discount.
- Never explain how molds are made or reveal size/format limits; quote per project.
- Every closed corporate deal gets asked for a testimonial + referral (the
  Archistruct quote is doing heavy lifting — collect five more).
